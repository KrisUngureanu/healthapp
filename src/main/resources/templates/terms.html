1. Service Description

HealthApp is a closed platform for elite sports medical teams. Team physicians (“Physician”) invite athletes (“Athlete”) to share data collected via integrations with Oura, Polar, Garmin, and WHOOP (“Integrations”). The Service aggregates metrics (e.g., sleep, HR/HRV, SpO₂, activity, workouts) and visualizes them for assigned Physicians to support training and recovery decisions.

Medical Disclaimer. The Service is not a medical device, does not diagnose conditions, and does not replace a medical consultation. All health decisions remain the responsibility of Physicians and Athletes.
